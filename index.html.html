About Us

Expertise You Can Trust

Our Philosophy

At the Cardiovascular Centre Norwood, we believe that exceptional cardiac care begins well before a crisis occurs. Our philosophy is rooted in proactive, preventative wellness — combining the precision of advanced diagnostics with the warmth of a patient-centred practice.

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of clinical excellence, maintaining accreditation with the Cardiac Society of Australia and New Zealand and regularly participating in international research collaborations.

Every patient who walks through our doors receives a personalised care plan — not a protocol. We listen, we explain, and we partner with you on your journey to better heart health.

"We don't just treat hearts — we protect the people behind them."
— Dr. Prash, Founding Cardiologist

Behind the Scenes

A Patient Communication System Built for the Modern Clinic

Every touchpoint in your care journey is automated — eliminating the gaps that frustrate patients and burden your staff.

New Booking Submitted INSTANT

The moment you submit a booking, an SMS and calendar invite are sent to your phone — no waiting, no uncertainty.

Trigger: Form submission → Action: SMS + Email + Calendar invite

Referral Uploaded < 2 HRS

Your referral is instantly routed to the triage nurse. You receive a confirmation: "We've received your referral and will review it within 24 hours."

Trigger: Document upload → Action: Nurse alert + Patient auto-reply

24-Hour Reminder AUTOMATED

A personalised reminder arrives the day before your appointment with a single tap to confirm or reschedule — keeping the schedule optimised for everyone.

Trigger: 24h before appointment → Action: Confirm / Reschedule SMS

Post-Visit Check-In 7 DAYS

A private feedback survey is sent one week after your visit. Any concerns are flagged directly to the clinical team before they escalate.

Trigger: 7 days post-appointment → Action: Private satisfaction survey
